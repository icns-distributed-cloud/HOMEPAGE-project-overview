    About Us

    We are researching and developing a new paradigm, distributed cloud. The distributed cloud can help provide faster, more reliable and energy-efficient cloud services. We are concentrating on this :)

    Our Mission

    Development of distributed cloud ecosystem model and platform prototype, Support of real-time for high-performance computing, Performance profiling and job scheduling based on multi-objectives

    Our Plan

    DC platform prototype development with service mobility support technology, Cloud service development in distributed cloud and service packaging, Open SW release from github

    Our Vision

    Leading of standardization for distributed cloud computing, Securing of the core technology for mobility in distributed cloud computing
